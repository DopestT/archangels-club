The platform

Exclusivity is the product.

Archangels Club is not open to the public. Every member is manually reviewed and approved before gaining access. Every creator application is vetted. Every piece of content goes through a moderation queue before it goes live. The result is a platform where quality is protected by design — not by algorithm.

Revenue streams

Four ways to earn.

Creators on Archangels Club aren't limited to a single revenue channel. Every interaction is a potential transaction.

Content Unlocks

Fans pay a one-time fee to access a specific piece of locked content — photo, video, audio, or written post.

Subscriptions

Monthly recurring access at a price set by the creator. Active subscribers get configurable discounts on individual content.

Tips

Direct fan-to-creator payments with no strings attached. A frictionless way for fans to show appreciation.

Custom Requests

Fans describe and price a personalized content request. Creators accept or decline. Premium, one-to-one monetization.

Operations

Full-stack admin control.

The admin layer gives operators complete visibility and control over every aspect of the platform.

Member Approval Queue

Every signup requires manual admin approval before access is granted. Rejection and suspension are permanent controls.

Content Moderation

All content is reviewed before going live. Admins can approve, reject, request changes, or schedule for future publication.

Creator Applications

Creators apply and are approved independently from member access. Separate vetting protects platform quality.

Platform Intelligence

Admin dashboard surfaces revenue signals, high-potential creators, churn risk, and moderation pressure in real time.

Review Moderation

Fan reviews require admin approval before publishing. No unvetted content reaches creator profiles.

Compliance Infrastructure

Age verification, DMCA handling, content reporting, privacy policy, and terms of service fully built in.
